Welcome to the RPG Fantasy Football League!

Embark on an epic adventure where fantasy meets football. Choose your character class, draft your team, and use unique abilities to conquer your opponents. Let the games begin!

League Rules

League Structure

1. Teams: The league will consist of 18 teams, each representing a fantasy character class (e.g., Warrior, Mage, Rogue).

2. Draft: A traditional snake draft will be held to select NFL players for each team's roster.

Character Classes and Abilities

1. Warrior

Mighty Blow: Once per week, select a player who will gain an additional 2 points for every 20 rushing yards.

Iron Will: Adds 2 points to each rushing touchdown scored by your running backs.

2. Mage

Mystic Shield: Once per week, protect one player from negative points (e.g., turnovers, missed kicks).

Time Warp: Once per week, double the negative points from turnovers for one opponent’s player in a specific position.

3. Rogue

Sneak Attack: Once per week, your tight end receives an additional 0.5 points per reception.

Shadow Step: Your flex spot tight end scores 1.5x points.

4. Cleric

Revive: Once per week, start a player in the IR spot in the flex position and gain 7 points.

Sanctuary: Once per week, prevent a player from being affected by opponent abilities.

5. Hunter

Sniper Shot: Once per week, select a wide receiver or tight end whose score will increase by 1 point for every 20 receiving yards.

Hunting Party: Gain an additional 7 points if both your starting wide receivers score a touchdown.

6. Paladin

Righteous Fury: Once per week, add 2 points for every 50 rushing or receiving yards gained by your RBs and WRs.

Divine Retribution: Add 5 points to any player who scores a touchdown of 30 yards or more.

7. Bard

Song of Victory: Once per week, change your kicker before the Monday night game.

Encore: Double the points of every field goal made by your kicker over 50 yards.

8. Necromancer

Soul Harvest: For every 1 point you take off of your score, you gain the same amount of FAAB. Must be submitted before the game starts (max of 10 points).

Dark Pact: Bench your flex player and gain 3 points and 5 FAAB.

9. Druid

Earth’s Embrace: Gain 15 points if your total bench score exceeds 100 points in a week.

Wild Growth: Gain 1 point for each touchdown scored by your bench players.

10. Sorcerer

Arcane Wealth: Gain an additional 5 FAAB for each player in your starting lineup who scores over 20 points.

Mystic Bargain: Double your FAAB earnings from weekly challenges and subtract 5 FAAB from any store purchase that week.

Player Rosters

1. Roster Size: Each team will have a roster consisting of the following positions: 1 QB, 2 RB, 2 WR, 1 TE, 1 FLEX (RB/WR/TE), 1 K, and 6 bench spots.

2. Lineup Submission: Lineups must be submitted before the start of each game week.

Matchups and Scoring

1. Weekly Matchups: Teams will compete in head-to-head matchups each week, with standard PPR (Points Per Reception) scoring rules applied.

2. Scoring: The league will use standard PPR scoring with additional points for using character abilities.

Waivers and Trades

1. FAAB System: Free Agent Acquisition Budget (FAAB) will be used for acquiring free agents, with teams bidding on available players.

2. Trades: Trades are allowed between all teams, subject to league approval.

Weekly Challenges (Weeks 1-17)

Week 1: Quick Start - Challenge: Highest combined score of QB, RB, and WR. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 2: Rushing Rampage - Challenge: Most rushing yards by RBs. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 3: Air Raid - Challenge: Most receiving yards by WRs. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 4: Tight End Triumph - Challenge: Most points by a TE. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 5: Flex Appeal - Challenge: Highest scoring FLEX player. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 6: Quarterback Quest - Challenge: Highest total points by QBs. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 7: Rookie Sensation - Challenge: Most points by a rookie player. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 8: Veteran’s Day - Challenge: Most points by players aged 30 and above. Reward: 10 FAAB points.

Week 9: Clutch Performance
